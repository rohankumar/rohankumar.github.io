Biography

I am a Graduate Student at the Langugage Technologies Institute, Carnegie Mellon University. I am broadly interested in Machine Learning for NLP. My advisor is Yiming Yang.

Previously, I developed machine learning models for Search as a Data Scientist at Flipkart (a Walmart company), India. Before that, I was a Visiting Research Scholar at the Computer Science Dept, CMU studying online user satisfaction and anomalous behaiour, advised by Christos Faloutsos.
